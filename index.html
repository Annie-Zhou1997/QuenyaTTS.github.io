Quenya TTS

This project is a Quenya Text-To-Speech (TTS) created using IMS-Toucan.
It is a master's thesis project for the MSc in Voice Technology at the University of Groningen.
Here are some pre-generated audio demos.

In this project, a 30-minute Quenya dataset was fine-tuned on Finnish, English, and IMS-Toucan's multilingual models. The Finnish and English models were trained from scratch using the CSS10 Finnish and LJSpeech datasets respectively. For the LJSpeech dataset, only 10:30 hours of data were used to match the duration of the CSS10 dataset. The real voice for Quenya is provided by Glǽmscrafu.

"Even in a lamp, it doesn't go to waste, and forests do have value too."

2. Mutta tuvassa poltetaan lamppua, sanoi isä.

"But in the cottage, we burn a lamp, said the father."

3. Olin nähnyt unta, että isä yöllä pani öljyä lamppuun, ja että se sitten koko päivän paloi.

"I had dreamed that father put oil in the lamp at night, and that it then burned all day."
