Quenya TTS

This project is a Quneya Text-To-Speech (TTS) created using IMS-Toucan.

It is a master's thesis project for the MSc in Voice Technology at the University of Groningen.

Here are some pre-generated audio demos.

In this study, a 30-minute Quneya dataset was fine-tuned on Finnish, English, and IMS-Toucan's multilingual models. The Finnish and English models were trained from scratch using the CSS10 Finnish and LJSpeech datasets respectively. For the LJSpeech dataset, only 10:30 hours of data were used to match the duration of the CSS10 dataset.

Finnish

lampussakaan hukkaan mene, ja maksavathan ne metsätkin.
Even in a lamp, it doesn't go to waste, and forests do have value too.

Mutta tuvassa poltetaan lamppua, sanoi isä.
But in the cottage, we burn a lamp, said the father.

Olin nähnyt unta, että isä yöllä pani öljyä lamppuun, ja että se sitten koko päivän paloi.
I had dreamed that father put oil in the lamp at night, and that it then burned all day.
